Wireframes / Sketching

I decided to redesign the homepage of www.ftdcompanies.com as a way to showcase my skills and illustrate my design process. First after initial discovery, I sketch out my ideas and wireframe the layout helping me to establish the visual hierarchy.

View Wireframe

Mockups

After wireframes, I'll move into creating mockups. In this phase I establish color, typography, imagery and content. I designed this page in Sketch, but I am also experienced in Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma, and Invision.

View Mockup

Prototypes

After the mockups are created, I take the designs and turn them into a working prototypes using HTML5, CSS3, and presentational JS. This phase also includes responsive design and decisions to allow for flexible content. After this phase, the layouts will be ready to be sent to back-end development for integration.
