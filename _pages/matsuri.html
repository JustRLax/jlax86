Super D Matsuri 2018

Super D Matsuri 2018 took place on April 14th-15th 2018 in Apple Valley, CA at Grange Motor Circuit. This event featured drift masters traveling from Japan (Naoki Nakamura, Miki Takagi, Mitsuyoshi Nishio) to join drivers from around North America.
